ABOUT US

Smt. Manorama Maharana was Born in 1931 at Nadia Santipur of West Bengal. With the passage of time, she was destined to get married to Dr. Gour Hari Maharana of Orissa. She was gifted with great artistic calibre. She was intensively in love with music and it was the only way, through she tried to realize her own inner self. Even if there was a pause for sometime, for worldly duties, she could bring the vibrations of her feelings. Emotional attachments towards art and culture remained throughout her motherhood. She was the renowned mother of 5 sons and 3 daughters. She encouraged her family members towards acheiving their goals and things beyond the perception of a common mind. It is her blessings, endeavours and consistent sadhna that har resulted in the fame acheived by two of her sons and daugthers, not only in India but also in abroad.

Mr. Ganga Narayan Maharana who was born in 1967 hails from the state of Orissa. Since his childhood he nurtured a dream of painting and making innovations in art. Inspite of some initial resistance for his family he pursued art wth passion, as he got constant inspiration from his mother Smt. Manorama Maharana. He has many solo exhibitions to his credit and group shows at India Habitat Centre, Triveni Art Centre, Lalit Kala Academy and many more prestigious art galleries. Presently he is working as Art teacher at Apeejay School. He guides many new budding artists to achieve their goals. Many of his students get selected in Fashion Design and Interior College of Art and Polytechnique.
